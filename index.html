Research Areas

Structural Vibration Control

Development of advanced control systems for mitigating structural vibrations in buildings and bridges under dynamic loading conditions.

  • Active vibration control systems
  • Passive damping technologies
  • Semi-active control devices
  • Tuned mass damper design
  • Magnetorheological dampers

Seismic Engineering

Comprehensive analysis and evaluation of structural response to seismic events, with focus on performance-based design methodologies.

  • Seismic response analysis
  • Earthquake-resistant design
  • Structural vulnerability assessment
  • Performance-based seismic design
  • Ground motion characterization

Smart Structural Systems

Integration of advanced sensing technologies and IoT solutions for intelligent structural monitoring and damage detection.

  • Structural health monitoring
  • Real-time damage detection
  • Wireless sensor networks
  • AI-based diagnostic systems
  • Digital twin technologies

Experimental Research

State-of-the-art experimental facilities for validating theoretical models and testing innovative structural systems.

  • Shake table testing
  • Hybrid simulation
  • Field monitoring programs
  • Scale model experiments
  • Material characterization
