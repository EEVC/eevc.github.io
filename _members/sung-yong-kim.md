Prof. Sung-Yong Kim leads the Earthquake Engineering and Vibration Control
(EEVC) Lab at Changwon National University, where he serves as research
director since 2021. He earned his B.S. (2008), M.S. (2010), and Ph.D.
(2017) degrees from Seoul National University.

His research bridges experimental testing, advanced nonlinear finite element
analysis, and data-driven methods to advance the seismic and vibration
performance of buildings and their structural and nonstructural components.
Current interests span vibration control, seismic design, structural health
monitoring, steel and composite structures, and fire design — applied to
civil defense shelters, composite moment-frame connections, braced frame
optimization, and vibration-sensitive facilities.
