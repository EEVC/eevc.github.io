title:          "Peak Response of Frictional Tuned Mass Dampers Optimally Designed to White Noise Base Acceleration"
date:           2019-02-15 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing"
pub_date:       "2019"
abstract: >-
  Stochastic responses of a primary structure equipped with frictional tuned
  mass dampers (FTMDs) optimally designed to white-noise base acceleration
  are investigated. A statistical linearization technique replaces the
  nonlinear Coulomb friction force with a statistically equivalent linear one,
  and a statistical nonlinearization technique is then adopted to improve
  accuracy in predicting the root-mean-square FTMD response. A correction
  factor is proposed together with an explicit formula for the peak response
  factor, and numerical simulations confirm the accuracy of both factors.
authors:
