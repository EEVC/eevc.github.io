title:          "Cyclic Seismic Performance of Weak-Axis RBS Welded Steel Moment Connections"
date:           2019-10-15 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "International Journal of Steel Structures"
pub_date:       "2019"
abstract: >-
  Cyclic seismic performance of weak-axis reduced beam section (RBS) welded
  steel moment connections is experimentally investigated. Connections
  designed using the procedure proposed by Gilton and Uang exhibited
  excellent seismic performance well beyond a 4% story drift while retaining
  more than 80% of the beam plastic capacity. The sharp corner of the
  C-shaped fillet weld used to attach the beam web to the shear plate can act
  as a stress concentration that initiates crack propagation, and slight
  trimming of the sharp corner is recommended to mitigate the notch effect.
