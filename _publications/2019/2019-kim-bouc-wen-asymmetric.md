title:          "Description of Asymmetric Hysteretic Behavior Based on the Bouc-Wen Model and Piecewise Linear Strength-Degradation Functions"

pub:            "Engineering Structures"
pub_date:       "2019"
abstract: >-
  The Bouc-Wen model is widely used to describe hysteretic behavior, but
  capturing asymmetric hysteresis with strength degradation remains
  challenging. Previous Bouc-Wen variants are first reviewed, and an extended
  formulation is then proposed in which the original Bouc-Wen model is
  combined with piecewise linear functions that capture asymmetric strength
  degradation. The proposed model is verified against cyclic seismic test
  results of welded steel moment connections with composite floor slabs,
  reproducing the asymmetric, degrading hysteresis observed experimentally.
