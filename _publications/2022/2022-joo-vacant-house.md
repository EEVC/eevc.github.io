abstract: >-
  Factors affecting the occurrence of vacant houses are identified by use area
  (residential vs. commercial) and their statistical influence is examined.
  Residential, population, social, physical-environment, and geographic factors
  are significant for residential areas, while individual building factors
  dominate in commercial areas, informing sustainable community regeneration.
