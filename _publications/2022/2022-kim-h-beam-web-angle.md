title:          "Flexural Performance of H-Beam Strengthened by Bolting the Web and Double Angles"
date:           2022-04-30 00:01:00 +0900

pub:            "Journal of the Regional Association of Architectural Institute of Korea"
pub_date:       "2022"
abstract: >-
  An economical retrofit that bolts longitudinal double angles to the web of
  H-beams is evaluated through full-scale tests and finite element analysis. The
  reinforced beams achieved at least a 14% strength increase and retained
  strength up to about 6% end rotation, with web-reinforcement angles
  contributing to both stiffness and strength.
