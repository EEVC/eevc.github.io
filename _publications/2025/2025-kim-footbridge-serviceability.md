abstract: >-
  A probabilistic, measurement-based serviceability methodology is proposed for
  lightweight long-span pedestrian walkways. Site-specific discomfort thresholds
  and a weighted MTVV approach are introduced and validated on a 52-m span
  footbridge through long-term ambient monitoring and controlled resonance
  experiments, improving accuracy over conservative standard limits.
