pub:            "Engineering Structures"
pub_date:       "2025"
abstract: >-
  A neural-network hysteresis model that directly generates hysteresis loops
  from data, without differential-equation parameter identification, is trained
  on Bouc-Wen and Bouc-Wen-Noori numerical data and experimental composite
  connection and brace data. A dataset-augmentation scheme improves robustness,
  accurately reproducing asymmetric and pinched loops while reducing
  computational effort.
