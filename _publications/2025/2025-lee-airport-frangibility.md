abstract: >-
  An equivalent aircraft-impact load estimation method that extends the
  classical Riera approach by incorporating structure-impactor interaction is
  proposed for frangibility assessment of airport facilities. The model is
  validated through finite element analyses, providing a practical
  intermediate-level tool that avoids full-scale experiments and high-fidelity
  simulations.
