pub:            "Journal of Constructional Steel Research"
pub_date:       "2024"
abstract: >-
  Floor-vibration serviceability of a two-story piloti-supported modular
  educational building is evaluated through heel-drop tests, ambient vibration
  monitoring, and random walking tests. Gaps between the steel frame and modular
  unit beams caused excessive floor flexibility, and modular-support retrofit
  schemes are proposed and verified to improve serviceability.
