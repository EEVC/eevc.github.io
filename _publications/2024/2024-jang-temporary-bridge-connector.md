abstract: >-
  Structural details of a hinge connection ("speedBridge") for rapidly assembled
  steel girder segments are developed and verified by testing and FEA. The
  connection developed 5% rad plastic rotation without fracture and reached a
  maximum load of 110% of the beam plastic moment, with FEA confirming the load
  transfer path.
