---
title:          "Enhancing Robustness of Floor Vibration Control by Using Asymmetric Tuned Mass Damper"
date:           2014-06-30 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of Korean Society of Steel Construction"
pub_date:       "2014"
abstract: >-
  When floor vibration problems occur in existing buildings, a tuned mass damper
  (TMD) is a viable solution but becomes inefficient when the natural frequency
  changes due to uncontrollable variation of floor mass — a phenomenon known as
  TMD off-tuning. This study proposes an asymmetric TMD with two asymmetric
  linear springs that dissipates floor vibrational energy through both
  translational and rotational motion. An easy-to-use graphical optimization
  method was developed. The proposed asymmetric TMD outperformed the
  conventional TMD by 28% in vibration control and provided about twice the
  robustness against frequency uncertainty.
