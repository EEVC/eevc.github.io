title:          "Effective Impulse Model for Prediction of Vibration Response of High-Frequency Steel Staircases"
date:           2016-11-15 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of Constructional Steel Research"
pub_date:       "2016"
abstract: >-
  Steel staircases used in reinforced-concrete building structures typically
  exhibit high-frequency, low-damping characteristics and are subjected to
  footfall forces whose magnitude and pace differ from those of building
  floors. Existing footfall force models are reviewed and evaluated against
  full-scale staircase test data, and a new effective footfall impulse model
  is proposed for predicting the maximum acceleration response of
  high-frequency steel staircases. The proposed effective-impulse formula
  achieves about 10% average error and outperforms the SCI P354 model
  developed for floors.
