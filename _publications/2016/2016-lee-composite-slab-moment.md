title:          "Investigation of Composite Slab Effect on Seismic Performance of Steel Moment Connections"
date:           2016-02-28 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of Constructional Steel Research"
pub_date:       "2016"
abstract: >-
  Concerns have been raised that the presence of a concrete floor slab and the
  resulting composite action may compromise the strong-column / weak-beam
  philosophy of seismic design. This study experimentally and analytically
  investigates how the degree of composite action between the steel beam and
  concrete slab affects the cyclic seismic response of welded steel moment
  connections, providing evidence relevant to seismic design and assessment
  of existing steel moment frames.
