title:          "Strain Compatibility Method for the Design of Short Rectangular Concrete-Filled Tube Columns Under Eccentric Axial Loads"
date:           2016-09-15 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Construction and Building Materials"
pub_date:       "2016"
abstract: >-
  An empirical and practical stress-strain model for confined concrete is
  developed for use with the strain compatibility method of ACI 318-14, AISC
  360-10, and Eurocode 4 in designing short rectangular CFT columns under
  eccentric axial loads. An updated database of test results is assembled to
  evaluate existing provisions and stress-strain models for concrete, and a
  new concrete stress-strain model is proposed that yields more accurate and
  consistent predictions of the P-M interaction strength of rectangular CFT
  columns over a broad range of design conditions.
