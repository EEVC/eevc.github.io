title:          "Damage Potential Analysis and Earthquake Engineering-Related Implications of Sep. 12, 2016 M5.8 Gyeongju Earthquake"
date:           2016-12-30 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of the Earthquake Engineering Society of Korea"
pub_date:       "2016"
abstract: >-
  Damage potential of the September 12, 2016 M5.8 Gyeongju earthquake is
  analyzed from a structural engineering perspective. Recorded ground motions
  are interpreted in terms of intensity measures and elastic and inelastic
  single-degree-of-freedom response, with implications for the seismic design
  and assessment of buildings on the Korean Peninsula — historically considered
  a region of low seismicity.
