title:          "Nondimensionalized Bouc-Wen Model with Structural Degradation for Kalman Filter-Based Real-Time Monitoring"
date:           2021-10-15 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Engineering Structures"
pub_date:       "2021"
abstract: >-
  The Bouc-Wen model is widely accepted for describing seismic hysteretic
  behavior, but in structural monitoring applications its phenomenological
  formulation often introduces redundant parameters that cause over-fitting.
  The Bouc-Wen model is reexamined and recast in a nondimensionalized form
  in which the hysteretic behavior is described in terms of the yield
  displacement and the nondimensional hysteresis displacement is bounded
  between -1 and 1. In the proposed model the dissipated energy is nearly
  equivalent to the displacement per cycle and can be interpreted as
  dissipation per equivalent number of cycles, giving physical meaning to
  the per-cycle degradation. The applicability of the proposed model is
  experimentally validated using an unscented Kalman filter-based parameter
  identification technique.
