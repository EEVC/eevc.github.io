title:          "Optimum Design of Linear Multiple Tuned Mass Dampers Subjected to White-Noise Base Acceleration Considering Practical Configurations"
date:           2018-09-15 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Engineering Structures"
pub_date:       "2018"
abstract: >-
  Optimum design and analysis of linear multiple tuned mass dampers (MTMDs)
  with various practical configurations are presented for a single-degree-of-
  freedom primary structure subjected to a zero-mean white-noise base
  acceleration. Six practical configurations — constrained with linearly
  distributed frequency ratios, uniformly distributed damping coefficients,
  linearly distributed mass ratios, and combinations thereof — are formulated
  and comparatively analyzed using two different optimization techniques. The
  results provide simple, design-oriented guidance for selecting MTMD
  parameters in practical applications.
