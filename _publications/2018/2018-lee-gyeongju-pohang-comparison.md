title:          "Comparative Analysis of Structural Damage Potentials Observed in the 9.12 Gyeongju and 11.15 Pohang Earthquakes"
date:           2018-05-30 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of the Earthquake Engineering Society of Korea"
pub_date:       "2018"
abstract: >-
  A comparative analysis of the 9.12 Gyeongju and 11.15 Pohang earthquakes is
  conducted to provide plausible explanations for the damage observed in the
  Pohang event from both earthquake- and structural-engineering perspectives.
  Although damage potentials such as Arias intensity and effective peak
  ground acceleration were generally weaker in the Pohang earthquake, the
  spectral power of the PHA2 record at a soft-soil site was highly
  concentrated near 2 Hz with base shear reaching as high as 40% of building
  weight — a frequency band very close to the fundamental frequency of the
  piloti-type buildings severely damaged in northern Pohang.
