title:          "Review of Structural Design Provisions of Rectangular Concrete Filled Tubular Columns"
date:           2013-08-30 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of Korean Society of Steel Construction"
pub_date:       "2013"
abstract: >-
  As a preliminary study toward unified standards for composite structures, the
  structural provisions for rectangular CFT columns in the 2005/2010 AISC
  Specification, ACI 318-08, and Eurocode 4 are comparatively analyzed,
  including nominal strength, confinement effect, plate slenderness, effective
  flexural stiffness, and material strength limitations.
