title:          "Analysis and Optimization of Multiple Tuned Mass Dampers with Coulomb Dry Friction"
date:           2020-04-15 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Engineering Structures"
pub_date:       "2020"
abstract: >-
  Optimum design and analysis of frictional multiple tuned mass dampers
  (FMTMDs) are presented, with Coulomb dry friction adopted as the energy
  dissipation mechanism. Statistical linearization is used to replace the
  nonlinear friction force with an equivalent linear one, and the optimum
  design that minimizes the root-mean-square displacement of the primary
  structure is identified. The equivalent damping ratio of a TMD unit
  decreases significantly with the number of TMDs. Four practical
  configurations — constrained on the frequency ratios and the coefficient of
  friction — are formulated and compared, and a particular solution
  describable with only three parameters is recommended for design because of
  its simplicity and accuracy.
