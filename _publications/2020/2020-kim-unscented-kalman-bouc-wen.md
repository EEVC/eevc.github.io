title:          "Constrained Unscented Kalman Filter for Structural Identification of Bouc-Wen Hysteretic System"
date:           2020-09-15 00:01:00 +0900

pub:            "Advances in Civil Engineering"
pub_date:       "2020"
abstract: >-
  A constrained unscented Kalman filter (CUKF) is adopted for state and
  parameter estimation of the Bouc-Wen model to overcome over-fitting issues
  associated with redundant parameters. The CUKF combines a constrained
  minimization technique with the conventional UKF so that predicted
  parameters can be bounded by physically reasonable constraints, yielding
  more accurate estimates than the unconstrained UKF. Comparative results
  show that the proposed CUKF is robust and efficient for system parameter
  identification of nonlinear hysteretic systems, supporting earthquake
  damage identification of civil structures.
