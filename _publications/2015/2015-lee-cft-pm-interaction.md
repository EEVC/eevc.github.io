---
title:          "Constitutive Relation of Concrete to Predict P-M Interaction Strength of Rectangular CFT Short Columns"
date:           2015-02-28 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of Korean Society of Steel Construction"
pub_date:       "2015"
abstract: >-
  The plastic stress distribution method and the strain compatibility method
  are the two representative methods for calculating the P-M interaction
  strength of rectangular concrete-filled tube (RCFT) columns. Because of the
  lack of an accurate constitutive model for concrete confined by a
  rectangular steel tube, current strain-compatibility provisions do not
  satisfactorily predict the P-M interaction strength under various material
  combinations. An empirical constitutive model that captures the stress-strain
  behavior of confined concrete in RCFT columns is proposed by defining the
  concrete crushing strain as a function of the steel-to-concrete strength
  ratio and the width-to-thickness ratio of the steel tube, leading to more
  accurate and consistent P-M interaction strength predictions.
authors:
