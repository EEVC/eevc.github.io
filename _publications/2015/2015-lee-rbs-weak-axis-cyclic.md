title:          "Cyclic Seismic Performance of RBS Weak-Axis Welded Moment Connections"
date:           2015-12-30 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of Korean Society of Steel Construction"
pub_date:       "2015"
abstract: >-
  Cyclic seismic performance of weak-axis reduced beam section (RBS) welded
  steel moment connections is investigated experimentally. Test results show
  that connections designed using the procedure proposed by Gilton and Uang
  develop excellent seismic performance well beyond a 4% story drift while
  retaining at least 80% of the beam plastic capacity, although stress
  concentration at the sharp corner of the C-shaped fillet weld between beam
  web and shear plate can initiate crack propagation and warrants slight corner
  trimming.
