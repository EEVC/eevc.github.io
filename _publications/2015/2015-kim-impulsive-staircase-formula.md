title:          "Formula for Equivalent Impulsive Force to Predict Vibrational Response of High-Frequency Staircases"
date:           2015-04-30 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of Korean Society of Steel Construction"
pub_date:       "2015"
abstract: >-
  An equivalent impulsive force formula is proposed for predicting the peak
  vibrational response of high-frequency steel staircases under footfall
  excitation. The formula is calibrated against full-scale staircase test data
  and improves prediction accuracy for high-frequency, low-damping stair
  systems whose footfall characteristics differ markedly from those of
  conventional building floors.
