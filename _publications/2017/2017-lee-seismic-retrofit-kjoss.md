title:          "Seismic Retrofit of Welded Steel Moment Connections Considering the Presence of Composite Floor Slabs"
date:           2017-02-28 00:01:00 +0900
selected:       false
pub:            "Journal of Korean Society of Steel Construction"
pub_date:       "2017"
abstract: >-
  Seismic retrofit of welded steel moment connections is studied with explicit
  consideration of the composite action induced by an overlying concrete floor
  slab. Building on prior tests of conventional moment connections with
  composite slabs, retrofit schemes that strengthen or modify the beam bottom
  flange and web are evaluated experimentally for their ability to relieve
  composite-action effects and restore satisfactory cyclic seismic
  performance.
authors:
